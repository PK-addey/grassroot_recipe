Ademe detsi

Description

Ademe detsi is the Ewe rendition of jute mallow soup in English. It is a dish common to West Africa. Traditionally it is made with a base of fish and seafood flavoured with fermented fish and lots of onions, pepper and red palm oil. The main ingredient of this dish is the jute mallow leaves that give this dish its signature texture and taste.

Ingredients

  • Jute mallow leaves.
  • Fermented fish
  • Medium sized onions
  • Pepper
  • Crab
  • precooked meat of choice
  • Red palm oil
  • Salt
